Chicken & Courgette Stir Fry

4 servings720 cal • 50g P • 76g C • 18g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 800g boneless chicken thighs, sliced thin (~1cm strips)
  • 2 medium courgettes, cut into half-moons
  • 200g green beans, trimmed and halved
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3cm fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 spring onions to garnish
  • 300g brown rice (prep separately in IP)
  • Marinade: 1 tbsp soy sauce + 1 tsp cornflour
  • Sauce: 3 tbsp oyster sauce, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • Slurry: 1 tsp cornflour + 2 tbsp cold water
Method
  1. Toss sliced chicken with marinade (soy + cornflour). Set aside 10–15 min
  2. Mix sauce in a small bowl. Set aside
  3. Heat wok over highest heat until smoking. Add 1 tbsp oil
  4. Add chicken in a single layer — sear 30 sec undisturbed, then stir fry 3–4 min until cooked through. Remove
  5. Add remaining oil, garlic and ginger — stir 30 sec (do not burn)
  6. Add green beans — stir fry 2 min. Add courgette — stir fry 2 min more. Both should have some colour
  7. Return chicken. Pour sauce over. Toss to combine
  8. Add cornflour slurry — stir until sauce thickens (1 min)
  9. Serve over rice. Garnish with spring onions. Vincent: extra rice to hit calorie target.

Mushroom Chicken & Green Beans

4 servings760 cal • 54g P • 78g C • 16g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 600g shredded batch chicken (second half of Tuesday batch)
  • 250g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  • 300g green beans, trimmed and halved
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2cm fresh ginger, grated
  • 300ml chicken stock (½ stock cube + water)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 spring onions to garnish
  • 300g brown rice (prep separately in IP)
Method
  1. Set IP to SAUTE, add oil. Cook onion 3 min. Add garlic and ginger — stir 1 min
  2. Add stock, soy sauce, oyster sauce, rice vinegar — stir to combine
  3. Add shredded chicken and mushrooms — stir through
  4. HIGH PRESSURE 3 minutes + quick release
  5. Set to SAUTE. Add green beans — simmer 4–5 min until just tender
  6. Drizzle sesame oil over. Season to taste
  7. Serve over rice. Garnish with spring onions

Batch Chicken Thighs (IP)

IP 25 min HP + 10 min NRMakes ~1.2kg shredded
⌄
Ingredients
  • 2kg boneless, skinless chicken thighs (defrosted overnight from frozen)
  • 500ml chicken stock (1 stock cube + water)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper
Method
  1. Add stock to Instant Pot
  2. Season chicken with garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper
  3. Place chicken in stock — can stack
  4. HIGH PRESSURE 25 minutes + 10 min natural release
  5. Shred with two forks in the pot
  6. Split into 2 equal portions (~600g each): one for Teriyaki Noodles tonight, refrigerate the second for Wednesday’s Mushroom Chicken

Salmon & Chickpea Coconut Curry

4 servings820 cal • 48g P • 82g C • 28g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 800g salmon fillets, skinless, cut into large chunks
  • 2 × 400g tins chickpeas, drained
  • 1 × 400ml tin full-fat coconut milk
  • 1 × 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 200g baby spinach
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2cm fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 300g brown rice (prep separately in IP)
Method
  1. SAUTE mode: heat oil, cook onions 3 min. Add garlic, ginger, curry powder, cumin — stir 1 min
  2. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, coconut milk, salt and pepper
  3. HIGH PRESSURE 3 minutes. Quick release
  4. SAUTE (low): lay salmon chunks on top. Simmer 5–6 min, turning once, until just cooked
  5. Gently break salmon into large flakes and stir through
  6. Stir in spinach until wilted. Season to taste
  7. Serve over rice

IP Beef Chilli

4 servings860 cal • 52g P • 88g C • 28g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 800g braising beef (chuck), diced into 2cm chunks
  • 2 × 400g tins chopped tomatoes
  • 1 × 400g tin kidney beans, drained
  • 1 × 400g tin baked beans
  • 1 red pepper, diced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp tomato puree
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 500ml beef stock (1 stock cube + water)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, salt and pepper
  • 300g brown rice (prep separately in IP)
Method
  1. SAUTE: brown beef in batches 2–3 min. Set aside
  2. Saute onion 3 min. Add garlic 1 min. Add cumin, paprika, chilli, oregano, tomato puree — stir 1 min
  3. Return beef. Add tomatoes, stock, red pepper, kidney beans, baked beans
  4. HIGH PRESSURE 35 minutes + 10 min natural release
  5. Season to taste. SAUTE 5 min to reduce if too thin
  6. Serve over rice

Mexican Chipotle Rice Bowl

4 servings800 cal • 44g P • 102g C • 20g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 700g boneless pork shoulder, cut into 4–5 large chunks
  • 1 × 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 × 400g tin kidney beans, drained
  • 200ml chicken stock (½ stock cube + water)
  • 2 tbsp chipotle paste
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, salt and pepper
  • 4 tbsp Greek yoghurt + lime wedges to serve
  • 300g brown rice (prep separately in IP)
Method
  1. SAUTE: season pork, brown 2–3 min each side. Remove
  2. Saute onion 2 min. Add garlic and chipotle paste — stir 1 min
  3. Return pork. Add tomatoes, stock, lime juice
  4. HIGH PRESSURE 50 minutes + 15 min natural release
  5. Remove pork, shred with two forks, return to pot
  6. SAUTE: add kidney beans, simmer 5 min. Season to taste
  7. Serve over rice with Greek yoghurt and lime wedges

Teriyaki Chicken Noodles

4 servings760 cal • 52g P • 82g C • 18g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 600g shredded batch chicken (first half of Tuesday batch)
  • 300g medium egg noodles (dry)
  • 2 heads broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 4 spring onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil, sesame seeds to garnish
  • Teriyaki sauce: 6 tbsp soy sauce, 4 tbsp honey, 2 tbsp mirin, 1 tsp sesame oil, 2 tsp cornflour + 2 tbsp cold water
Method
  1. Cook noodles per packet. Drain and set aside
  2. Mix all teriyaki sauce ingredients in a bowl
  3. High heat wok: add oil. Stir fry broccoli and carrots 3–4 min with some char
  4. Add shredded chicken, toss to heat through 2 min
  5. Pour sauce over. Toss to coat. Bubble 1–2 min until slightly thickened
  6. Add noodles, toss until fully combined
  7. Top with spring onions and sesame seeds. Vincent: add extra noodles if short on calories.

IP Asian Braised Mackerel

4 servings760 cal • 52g P • 80g C • 22g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 4 × 125g tins mackerel in brine, drained
  • 1 × 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 200ml chicken stock (½ stock cube + water)
  • 200g baby spinach
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2cm fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 spring onions to garnish
  • 300g brown rice (prep separately in IP)
Method
  1. SAUTE: heat oil, cook onion 3 min. Add garlic, ginger, chilli flakes — stir 1 min
  2. Add tomatoes, stock, soy sauce, rice vinegar — stir to combine
  3. Gently lay mackerel on top — do not stir or it will break
  4. HIGH PRESSURE 5 minutes + quick release
  5. SAUTE: stir in spinach until wilted. Drizzle sesame oil over
  6. Season to taste. Serve over rice. Garnish with spring onions
