IP Salmon & Chickpea Coconut Curry

Instant Pot4 servings820 cal/serving
▾
820 cal 48g pro 82g carb 28g fat
Ingredients (4 servings, with rice)
  • 800g salmon fillets, skinless, cubed
  • 2 tins chickpeas, drained
  • 1 tin coconut milk (400ml)
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 200g baby spinach
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2cm ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • 300g jasmine rice (to serve)
Method
  1. Set IP to SAUTE, add oil.
  2. Cook onions 3 min, add garlic, ginger, curry powder, cumin — stir 1 min.
  3. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, coconut milk, salt and pepper.
  4. Seal lid, HIGH PRESSURE 3 min.
  5. Quick release.
  6. Set to SAUTE (low), lay salmon on top.
  7. Simmer 5–6 min until just cooked.
  8. Break salmon into chunks, stir in spinach until wilted.
  9. Serve over rice.

Wok Pork & Pak Choi with Jasmine Rice

Wok4 servings790 cal/serving
▾
790 cal 46g pro 92g carb 16g fat
Ingredients (4 servings, with rice)
  • 700g pork mince
  • 3 heads pak choi, stems and leaves separated
  • 4 spring onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2cm ginger, grated
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 300g jasmine rice (to serve)
Method
  1. Cook rice.
  2. Heat wok until smoking, add oil.
  3. Sear pork mince in batches until browned.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, 1 min.
  5. Add soy and oyster sauce, toss.
  6. Add pak choi stems, 2 min.
  7. Add pak choi leaves and spring onions, 1–2 min.
  8. Drizzle sesame oil.
  9. Serve over rice.

IP Beef & Sweet Potato Stew

Instant Pot4 servings880 cal/serving
▾
880 cal 55g pro 86g carb 22g fat
Ingredients (4 servings, with rice)
  • 800g braising beef, 3cm chunks
  • 2 large sweet potatoes (~600g), cubed
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 200g fine green beans, trimmed
  • 1 red pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 500ml beef stock
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • 300g jasmine rice (to serve)
Method
  1. SAUTE — brown beef in batches, set aside.
  2. Saute onion 3 min, garlic 1 min, add paprika and thyme.
  3. Return beef, add tomatoes, stock, sweet potato, red pepper.
  4. HIGH PRESSURE 35 min.
  5. Natural release 10 min.
  6. SAUTE — add green beans, simmer 4–5 min.
  7. Season. Serve over rice.

Oven Chicken Thigh & Roasted Veg Sheet Pan

Oven4 servings610 cal/serving
▾
610 cal 52g pro 30g carb 24g fat
Ingredients (4 servings)
  • 8 bone-in skin-on chicken thighs (~1.2kg)
  • 400g baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 heads broccoli, florets
  • 2 red peppers, sliced
  • 1 red onion, wedges
  • 4 cloves garlic, skin on
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp Italian herbs
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 lemon
  • Salt & pepper
Method
  1. Preheat oven 200°C.
  2. Toss potatoes with oil, salt, pepper — roast 20 min.
  3. Season chicken with oil, herbs, paprika, salt, pepper.
  4. Add chicken, broccoli, peppers, onion, garlic to tray.
  5. Roast 40–45 min until golden and cooked through.
  6. Squeeze lemon over before serving.

Teriyaki Chicken Noodles

Wok4 servings760 cal/serving
▾
760 cal 52g pro 82g carb 18g fat
Ingredients (4 servings)
  • 800g shredded batch chicken thighs
  • 300g egg noodles (dry)
  • 2 heads broccoli, small florets
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 4 spring onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds
Teriyaki Sauce
  • 6 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp cornflour mixed with 2 tbsp cold water
Method
  1. Cook noodles, drain, set aside.
  2. Mix sauce.
  3. Wok — stir fry broccoli and carrots 3–4 min.
  4. Add chicken, heat through 2 min.
  5. Pour sauce, toss, bubble 1–2 min.
  6. Add noodles, toss.
  7. Top with spring onions and sesame seeds.

IP Turkey & Spinach Meatball Soup

Instant Pot4 servings720 cal/serving
▾
720 cal 54g pro 48g carb 16g fat
Meatball Ingredients
  • 800g turkey mince
  • 1 egg
  • 50g breadcrumbs
  • 30g Parmesan, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt & pepper
Soup Ingredients
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 800ml chicken stock
  • 1 tin cannellini beans, drained
  • 200g baby spinach
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 slices crusty bread per person (to serve)
Method
  1. Mix meatball ingredients, roll into ~20 balls.
  2. SAUTE — cook onion 3 min, garlic 1 min.
  3. Add tomatoes, stock, beans, seasoning.
  4. Gently lower meatballs in.
  5. HIGH PRESSURE 10 min.
  6. Quick release.
  7. Stir in spinach until wilted.
  8. Serve with crusty bread.

IP Beef Chilli

Instant Pot4 servings860 cal/serving
▾
860 cal 52g pro 88g carb 28g fat
Ingredients (4 servings, with rice & yoghurt)
  • Full batch beef mince base (~1.2kg, cooked)
  • 2 tins kidney beans, drained
  • 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 200g frozen sweetcorn
  • Salt & pepper
  • 300g jasmine rice (to serve)
  • 4 tbsp Greek yoghurt (to serve)
Method
  1. Cook rice.
  2. SAUTE — add mince base, kidney beans, spices.
  3. Simmer 10 min until thickened.
  4. Add sweetcorn, cook 3 min.
  5. Season.
  6. Serve over rice with Greek yoghurt.
