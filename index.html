Beef & Broccoli Stir Fry

WokPrep: 10 minCook: 12 min
▾
780 cal 50g pro 78g carb 24g fat
Ingredients (serves 2 — cook double)
  • 500g beef stir fry strips
  • 2 large heads broccoli, small florets
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb ginger, grated
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornflour + 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 150g rice per person (dry)
Method
  1. Mix soy, oyster sauce, sesame oil, cornflour slurry in a bowl
  2. Wok smoking hot, add veg oil
  3. Beef in single layer — don’t stir for 60 sec to sear
  4. Stir fry 2–3 min until browned, remove
  5. Splash more oil, broccoli 3–4 min
  6. Garlic + ginger 30 sec
  7. Return beef, pour sauce, toss 1–2 min until glossy
  8. Serve over rice

Teriyaki Chicken Noodles

WokPrep: 10 minCook: 15 min
▾
760 cal 52g pro 82g carb 18g fat
Ingredients (serves 2 — cook double)
  • 500g batch-cooked shredded chicken
  • 300g egg noodles (dry)
  • 2 pak choi, quartered
  • 1 red pepper, sliced
  • 2 spring onions, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb ginger, grated
Teriyaki Sauce
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornflour + 1 tbsp water
Method
  1. Cook noodles per packet, drain, toss with sesame oil
  2. Wok smoking hot, add veg oil
  3. Pak choi + pepper 2–3 min until slightly charred
  4. Garlic + ginger 30 sec
  5. Add chicken, toss 2 min
  6. Pour teriyaki sauce, stir fry 1–2 min until glossy
  7. Add noodles, toss everything together
  8. Top with spring onions

Garlic Chilli Pork Stir Fry

WokPrep: 10 minCook: 12 min
▾
800 cal 48g pro 76g carb 28g fat
Ingredients (serves 2 — cook double)
  • 600g pork loin steaks, sliced thin
  • 1 large courgette, halved and sliced
  • 1 red pepper, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 2 red chillies, sliced (deseed for less heat)
  • 1 bunch spring onions, cut 3cm
Sauce
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornflour + 2 tbsp water
Method
  1. Mix sauce ingredients in a bowl
  2. Wok smoking hot, add veg oil
  3. Pork single layer — 60 sec without stirring for colour
  4. Stir fry 3–4 min until cooked, remove
  5. Splash oil, courgette + pepper 3 min
  6. Garlic + chillies + spring onions 30 sec
  7. Return pork, pour sauce, toss 1–2 min
  8. Serve over rice with steamed broccoli

IP Chicken Curry

Instant PotPrep: 10 minCook: 15 min SAUTE
▾
820 cal 54g pro 85g carb 26g fat
Ingredients (serves 2)
  • 500g shredded chicken
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp curry paste
  • 100ml coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Handful of spinach
  • 150g rice (dry, for 2)
Method
  1. IP SAUTE: oil, onion 3 min, garlic 1 min
  2. Add curry paste, stir 1 min
  3. Add tomatoes and coconut milk
  4. Add chicken, SAUTE 3–4 min
  5. Stir in spinach until wilted
  6. Serve over rice with steamed broccoli

IP Asian Braised Mackerel

Instant PotPrep: 5 minCook: 10 min SAUTE
▾
760 cal 52g pro 80g carb 22g fat
Ingredients (serves 2) — ~3.6g omega-3 per serving
  • 4 tins mackerel in brine (145g each), drained
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp five spice
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 thumb ginger, sliced
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 100ml water
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Jasmine rice + steamed greens
Method
  1. Mix soy, dark soy, oyster sauce, five spice, vinegar, water
  2. IP SAUTE: garlic + ginger in oil, 1 min
  3. Pour sauce in, bring to simmer
  4. Add mackerel gently — don’t stir hard
  5. Simmer 5–7 min until sauce thickens
  6. Stir in sesame oil
  7. Serve over rice with steamed greens

Roast Chicken Thighs

IP + OvenPrep: 10 minCook: 15 min HP + 10 min oven
▾
840 cal 52g pro 72g carb 30g fat
Ingredients (serves 2)
  • 6 bone-in chicken thighs (skin on)
  • 4 medium potatoes, quartered
  • 200g frozen veg or carrots/peas
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Garlic powder, paprika, salt, pepper
  • 200ml chicken stock
Method
  1. Season thighs with spices
  2. IP SAUTE: sear skin-down 3 min
  3. Add stock and potatoes
  4. Seal → HIGH PRESSURE 15 min, quick release
  5. Optional: grill thighs 5–10 min to crisp
  6. Steam veg, serve with cooking liquid as gravy

Daily Supplements

Vitamin D3 — 1,000 IU daily, year-round. UK sunlight unreliable even in summer.
Fish Oil — 1g EPA+DHA daily. Mackerel covers Saturday only; this smooths omega-3 across the week.
Magnesium — 200–400mg citrate or glycinate daily. Pumpkin seeds only on 2 days; hard to hit 300mg RNI from food alone.

Watch Points

Michael’s fat % — At ~105g fat on 2,358 cal = 40%. Trim cheese or PB on his portions to bring closer to 35%.
Salt on wok nights — 3 tbsp soy sauce per recipe adds ~9g sodium. Use low-sodium soy or reduce to 2 tbsp where possible.
Fibre — ~28g vs 30g target. Add an extra portion of veg or switch to higher-fibre bread.
