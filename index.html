Batch Chicken Thighs

Instant Pot Prep: 5 min Cook: 25 min HP + 10 NR
▾
330 cal 52g pro 0g carb 12g fat
Ingredients (per 250g) — Makes ~1.6kg cooked
  • 2kg boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 500ml chicken stock (1 cube + water)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
Method
  1. Add stock to Instant Pot
  2. Season chicken with garlic powder, paprika, salt, pepper
  3. Place in stock (can stack)
  4. Seal lid → HIGH PRESSURE 25 min
  5. Natural release 10 min, then quick release
  6. Shred with two forks in the pot
  7. Store in containers — use within 4 days
batch

Batch Beef Mince Base

Instant Pot Prep: 10 min Cook: 20 min HP + 10 NR
▾
380 cal 42g pro 8g carb 20g fat
Ingredients (per 250g) — Makes ~1.2kg cooked
  • 1.5kg beef mince (20% fat — drain after browning)
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
Method
  1. IP on SAUTE → add oil
  2. Brown mince in batches, break it up, set aside
  3. Saute onions 3 min, garlic 1 min
  4. Return mince, add tomatoes + spices
  5. Seal → HIGH PRESSURE 20 min
  6. Natural release 10 min
  7. Split into portions for chilli/bolognese/bowls

Overnight Protein Oats

No Cook Prep: 5 min (night before) Cook: None
▾
720 cal 36g pro 82g carb 30g fat
Ingredients (per serving)
  • 80g porridge oats
  • 200ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 100g Greek yoghurt (0% fat)
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 30g pumpkin seeds — zinc, magnesium, omega-3 ALA
  • Pinch of cinnamon (optional)
Method
  1. Mix oats, milk, yoghurt, and pumpkin seeds in a jar
  2. Stir in cinnamon if using
  3. Seal and refrigerate overnight
  4. Morning: top with banana and peanut butter

Scrambled Eggs on Toast

5 min Prep: 2 min Cook: 5 min
▾
580 cal 40g pro 34g carb 30g fat
Ingredients (per serving)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 slices wholemeal toast
  • 30g cheddar, grated
  • Splash of milk
  • Knob of butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: spinach, tomato
Method
  1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, pepper
  2. Melt butter in pan over medium-low
  3. Pour eggs, stir gently with spatula
  4. When nearly set, fold in cheese (and spinach)
  5. Serve on toast

Instant Pot Chicken Curry

Instant Pot Prep: 10 min Cook: 10–15 min SAUTE
▾
820 cal 54g pro 85g carb 26g fat
Ingredients (per serving)
  • 500g shredded chicken (for 2)
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp curry paste
  • 100ml coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Handful of spinach
  • 150g rice (dry, for 2)
Method
  1. IP SAUTE: oil, onion 3 min, garlic 1 min
  2. Add curry paste, stir 1 min
  3. Add tomatoes and coconut milk
  4. Add shredded chicken
  5. SAUTE 3–4 min to heat through — no pressure cook needed
  6. Stir in spinach until wilted
  7. Serve over rice with steamed broccoli

IP Asian Braised Mackerel

Instant Pot Prep: 5 min Cook: 10 min SAUTE
▾
760 cal 52g pro 80g carb 22g fat
Ingredients (serves 2) — ~3.6g omega-3 & ~22 mcg vitamin D per serving
  • 4 tins mackerel in brine (145g each), drained
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp five spice powder
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 thumb ginger, sliced
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 100ml water
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 150g jasmine rice (dry, for 2) + steamed greens
  • Optional: kimchi
Method
  1. Mix soy, dark soy, oyster sauce, five spice, vinegar, water
  2. IP SAUTE: fry garlic and ginger in a little oil, 1 min
  3. Pour sauce in, bring to simmer
  4. Add drained mackerel gently — do not stir hard (it will flake)
  5. Simmer 5–7 min until heated through and sauce thickens
  6. Stir in sesame oil
  7. Serve over rice with steamed greens and optional kimchi
dinner

IP Braised Pork Belly

Instant Pot Prep: 10 min Cook: 35 min HP + 15 NR
▾
840 cal 50g pro 72g carb 34g fat
Ingredients (serves 2)
  • 600g pork belly, cut into 4cm chunks
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar or honey
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 thumb ginger, sliced
  • 200ml water
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Steamed rice + steamed broccoli
Method
  1. Optional: blanch pork in boiling water 3 min, drain
  2. Add pork and all ingredients except sesame oil to IP
  3. Seal → HIGH PRESSURE 35 min
  4. Natural release 15 min
  5. Remove pork. SAUTE to reduce sauce until thick and glossy
  6. Return pork, stir to coat, finish with sesame oil
  7. Serve over rice with steamed broccoli
