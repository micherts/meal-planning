Recipes ⌄
Batch — Sat 28 Mar

Batch Chicken Thighs (IP)

IP 25 min HP + 10 min NRMakes ~1.2kg shredded
⌄
Ingredients
  • 2kg boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 500ml chicken stock (1 stock cube + water)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper
Method
  1. Add stock to Instant Pot
  2. Season chicken with garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper
  3. Place chicken in IP on trivet or directly in stock
  4. HIGH PRESSURE 25 minutes + 10 min natural release
  5. Remove chicken, shred with two forks — discard liquid
  6. Split into two equal portions (~600g each): one for Chicken Curry, one for Teriyaki
Batch — Mon 30 Mar

Batch Pulled Pork Shoulder (IP)

IP 60 min HP + 15 min NRMakes ~900g pulled
⌄
Ingredients
  • 1.5kg boneless pork shoulder, cut into 4 large chunks
  • 400ml chicken stock (1 stock cube + water)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 thumb fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper
Method
  1. Mix marinade: soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, paprika, salt, pepper
  2. Add stock to IP. Place pork chunks in stock.
  3. Pour marinade over pork
  4. HIGH PRESSURE 60 minutes + 15 min natural release
  5. Remove pork and shred with two forks — keep some cooking liquid to moisten
  6. Split into two portions (~450g each): one for Asian Noodle Bowl, one for Mexican Rice Bowl

Salmon & Chickpea Coconut Curry

4 servings820 cal • 48g P • 82g C • 28g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 800g salmon fillets, skin off, cut into chunks
  • 2 × 400g tins chickpeas, drained
  • 2 × 400ml tins full-fat coconut milk
  • 1 × 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 200g baby spinach
  • 2 red peppers, sliced
  • 1 large white onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 thumb fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 300g jasmine rice (prep separately in IP)
Method
  1. SAUTE mode: heat oil, fry onion 3 min until softened
  2. Add garlic, ginger, curry powder, cumin, coriander — stir 1 min
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and deglaze the pot
  4. Add coconut milk, chickpeas, red peppers, salt and pepper — stir
  5. Lay salmon chunks on top — do not stir
  6. HIGH PRESSURE 4 minutes + quick release
  7. Open lid, stir gently — salmon will break into flakes naturally
  8. Stir through spinach until wilted. Add garam masala and lime juice.
  9. Serve over rice

Teriyaki Chicken Noodles

4 servings760 cal • 52g P • 82g C • 18g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 600g shredded chicken (from batch)
  • 400g medium egg noodles
  • 2 heads broccoli, cut into florets
  • 3 carrots, julienned
  • 4 spring onions, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 thumb fresh ginger, grated
  • 6 tbsp teriyaki sauce / mirin
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper
Method
  1. Cook noodles per packet — drain and set aside
  2. Mix sauce: teriyaki, soy sauce, rice vinegar, 1 tsp sesame oil
  3. High heat wok: heat vegetable oil, stir-fry broccoli and carrots 3–4 min
  4. Add garlic and ginger — stir 30 sec
  5. Add shredded chicken — toss to heat through
  6. Add noodles and pour over sauce — toss everything together 2 min
  7. Finish with remaining sesame oil and spring onions

  7. Finish with remaining sesame oil and spring onions

Turkey & Spinach Meatball Soup

4 servings720 cal • 54g P • 48g C • 16g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 800g turkey mince
  • 200g baby spinach
  • 400g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 litre chicken stock (2 stock cubes + water)
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 egg (for meatballs)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Crusty bread to serve
Method
  1. Mix turkey mince with egg, half the garlic, oregano, salt and pepper — form into 20 small meatballs
  2. SAUTE mode: heat oil, brown meatballs in batches 2 min each side — set aside
  3. Fry onion and carrot 3 min. Add remaining garlic and tomato puree — stir 1 min.
  4. Add chicken stock and deglaze. Add cherry tomatoes and chilli flakes.
  5. Return meatballs to pot.
  6. HIGH PRESSURE 8 minutes + quick release
  7. Stir through spinach until wilted. Adjust seasoning.
  8. Serve with crusty bread

Beef & Sweet Potato Stew

4 servings880 cal • 55g P • 86g C • 22g F
⌄
Ingredients
  • 800g diced braising beef
  • 600g sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 200g baby spinach
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 600ml beef stock (2 stock cubes + water)
  • 1 × 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (mixed with 2 tbsp water)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 300g jasmine rice (prep separately in IP)
Method
  1. SAUTE mode: heat oil, brown beef in batches 3–4 min — set aside
  2. Fry onion 3 min. Add garlic, tomato puree, paprika — stir 1 min.
  3. Add beef stock and deglaze. Add chopped tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce.
  4. Return beef. Add carrots and sweet potato.
  5. HIGH PRESSURE 35 minutes + 10 min natural release
  6. Stir through spinach until wilted
  7. If sauce too thin: SAUTE mode, stir in cornflour slurry, simmer 2 min
  8. Serve over rice
